The aim of my research is to alter the collaboration between individuals and machines. I work at the intersection of edge computing, machine learning, and computer vision. My main goal is to make AI systems that work well on real-world hardware that doesn't have a lot of resources, without losing speed or intelligence.

I am constantly working on new papers, making new systems, and coming up with new ideas that help this mission move forward. My current work is related to my project and involves multimodal perception, low-latency model optimisation, and hardware-software co-design for next-generation wearable devices. The goal is clear: to make AI that fits into people's lives and makes their lives better in a real, measurable way. It should be useful, reliable, and fast enough to seem invisible.
