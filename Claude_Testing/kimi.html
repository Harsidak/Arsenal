Main Characters

Harry Potter

The Boy Who Lived | Gryffindor

The protagonist who defeated the Dark Lord as a baby and continues to fight against evil.

Hermione Granger

The Brightest Witch | Gryffindor

Harry's brilliant friend known for her encyclopedic knowledge and quick thinking.

Ron Weasley

Loyal Friend | Gryffindor

Harry's loyal best friend from a large wizarding family, known for his humor and bravery.

Albus Dumbledore

Headmaster | Former Gryffindor

The wise and powerful headmaster of Hogwarts, guiding Harry in his fight against Voldemort.

Severus Snape

Potions Master | Former Slytherin

The complex Potions Master with a mysterious past and hidden loyalties.

Draco Malfoy

Rival | Slytherin

Harry's school rival from a prestigious pure-blood family.
